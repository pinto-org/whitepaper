%%%%%%%%%%%%%%% Default Liquidity Weight Function %%%%%%%%%%%%%%%

\subsubsection{$f^{\mathscr{W}^{\lambda'}}$}

We define the \term{Liquidity Weight} of $\lambda'$ as:

$$
    f^{\mathscr{W}^{\lambda'}} = 1 \times 10^8  \mkern6mu \text{(\textit{i.e.,} 100\%)}
$$
